THE EARLY JOURNEY

Early September,I embarked on my web development journey with a mix of excitement and nerves. W3Schools was my first guide, patiently walking me through the basics and helping me build a foundation. Then came FreeCodeCamp, where I really started to test my limits. Each challenge felt like a puzzle waiting to be solved. As a slow learner, progress didn’t always come quickly. There were moments of frustration—hours spent staring at lines of code that just wouldn’t work. But my resilience never wavered. I’d dig deeper, troubleshoot endlessly, and celebrate every breakthrough, no matter how small Now, just a few months later, I’ve come a long way. From fixing broken code to understanding concepts that once seemed impossible, I’m proud of my progress. This journey has taught me not just about web development, but also about perseverance and the power of staying the course. I still have much to learn, but I’m enjoying every step of the way.

LESSONS LEARNT DURING THE JOURNEY

  1. Persistence Pays Off: Slow progress is still progress, and resilience can overcome challenges.
  2. Debugging is Learning: Every error is an opportunity to understand your code better.
  3. Celebrate Small Wins: Small victories keep you motivated and show how far you’ve come.
  4. Resources Matter: Starting with the right tools, like W3Schools and FreeCodeCamp, sets a strong foundation.
  5. Learning is a Journey: Growth takes time, and enjoying the process makes it worthwhile.
